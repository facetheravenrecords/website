Introduction

序章解説

Mizu is fluidity — the art of adapting without losing shape. This project explored how digital design can mirror the qualities of water: clear, reflective, and constantly moving.

Goal

達成目標

The intention was to design an interface that feels alive, never static. We wanted users to sense motion even when nothing moves — a visual echo of still water holding infinite depth.

Procedure

実施手順

We worked with translucency, glass-like layers, and ripple-inspired motion. The interface reacts softly to user input, flowing from section to section. Mizu became a surface where form and reflection merge into one.
